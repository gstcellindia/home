⬆️ Promotion Entry

Select the officers being promoted from their current cadre, choose the cadre they're moving into, then set each officer's new seniority position (Sl. No.) in that cadre. Promoted officers are moved out of their old cadre's list and into the new one — vacancy counts and downloads update automatically.

Eligibility below is judged as on this date — entering a past date lets you record a promotion retrospectively, including for officers who have since retired but were still in service on this date.

📈 Promotion Estimator

Pick an officer to see their projected promotion path through every cadre above their current one — ASTO → DSTO → STO → DC → DCHG, and so on — not just the next rung. Each estimate accounts for the officers ahead of them in the queue, vacancies opening over time, and the fact that an officer further up might themselves be promoted out early (not just retire), freeing their seat sooner.

Step 1 — Download Template

CSV columns: sl_no, name, pen_no, order_no, feeder_category, dob (YYYY-MM-DD), retirement_age (56 or 60), status (leave blank for Auto, or use expired / relieved)
Status is normally calculated automatically from DOB and retirement age (Active / Retiring Soon / Retired). Only set this column to expired or relieved when that override applies.
