GOVERNMENT OF KERALA
Taxes (B) Department
NOTIFICATION
G.O.(P) No.66/2020/TAXES. Dated, Thiruvananthapuram, 14th May, 2020
31th Medam, 1195.

S.R.O. No.316/2020.— In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-rule (4) of rule 48 of the Kerala Goods and Services Tax Rules, 2017 (hereinafter referred as said rules), the Government of Kerala on the recommendations of the Council, and in supersession of the notification issued under G.O.(P) No. 218/2019/TAXES, dated 31st December, 2019 and published as S.R.O. No.1033/2019 in the Kerala Gazette Extraordinary No.3272 dated 31 st December, 2019, except as respects things done or omitted to be done before such supersession, hereby notifies registered person, other than a government department, a local authority, inserted [23/2021-Cetral Tax (S.R.O. 667/2021)] a Special Economic Zone unit and inserted [61/2020-Cetral Tax (S.R.O. 686/2020)]those referred to in sub-rules (2), (3), (4) and (4A) of rule 54 of the said rules, whose aggregate turnover in any preceding financial year from 2017-18 onwards a financial year (before amendment)[70/2020-Cetral Tax (S.R.O. 925/2020)] exceeds five crore rupees one hundred crore rupees (This was amended as under)

five hundred crore rupees [61/2020-Cetral Tax (S.R.O. 686/2020)] (further amended as under)

one hundred crore rupees [88/2020-Cetral Tax (S.R.O. 938/2020)] (further amended as under)

fifty crore rupees [5/2021-Cetral Tax (S.R.O. 351/2021)] (further amended as under)

twenty crore rupees [1/2022-Cetral Tax (S.R.O. 291/2022)] (further amended as under)

ten crore rupees [17/2022-Cetral Tax (S.R.O. 941/2022)] w.e.f. 01/10/2022 (before present amendment) [10/2022-Cetral Tax ( )], as a class of registered person who shall prepare invoice and other prescribed documents, in terms of sub-rule (4) of rule 48 of the said rules in respect of supply of goods or services or both to a registered person.

2. This notification shall come into force on the 1st October, 2020.

By order of the Governor,
R. RAJAGOPAL.,
Additional Secretary to Government.

Explanatory Note

(This does not form part of the notification, but is intended to indicate its general purport.)

On the recommendation of the Goods and Services Tax Council, the Government of Kerala have decided to exempt certain class of registered persons from issuing e-invoices and the date for implementation of e-invoicing extended to 1st day of October, 2020.

The notification is intended to achieve the above object.
