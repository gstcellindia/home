📊 Configure Heads for Budget

For each income head choose how it should be treated in the budget. If the computed Expenditure Budget works out to less than this period's actual income, every Changeable head simply gets a flat 5% increase over its actual amount (same treatment as Expenditure). Otherwise, Changeable heads absorb the increase/decrease needed to make the total Income Budget equal the total Expenditure Budget. Unchanged heads keep their actual value. You may also type a specific target value for any head.

For each expenditure head choose how it should be treated in the budget. Changeable heads are individually increased by 5% over their actual amount. Unchanged heads keep their actual value (no 5% increase). You may also type a specific target value for any head, which will be used as-is instead of the actual/5%-increase rule. The Expenditure Budget total is simply the sum of all included heads after applying these rules.
